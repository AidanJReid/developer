Marketing

I have worked in the tech sector for 14 years, including for a variety of startups and large corporates (Oracle, Salesforce, BMC Software, Vodafone, SAS, etc) in a variety of sales and marketing roles.

A thorough deep-dive of my career can be found on my Linkedin page. Some notable marketing highlights include:

  • Finalist in the Irish Blog Awards in 2017 for personal site.
  • Published five fiction novels, two of which were Amazon Bestsellers for their genre.
  • Consulted and advised hundreds of SME's on their SEO, blogging, content marketing and social media strategy.
  • Deep understanding of Inbound Marketing practices having worked for the industry leader Hubspot.
  • Diploma in Digital Marketing with strong knowledge of many methodologies (SEO, SEM, PPC, etc).
